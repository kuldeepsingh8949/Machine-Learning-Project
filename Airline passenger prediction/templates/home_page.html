Project

Passenger Details Satisfication Prediction Project.

The goal of this project is to develop a predictive model that assesses customer satisfaction levels for an airline.

By analyzing various factors that impact the passenger experience, the model aims to forecast satisfaction scores.Various machine learning algorithms (e.g., logistic regression, decision trees, random forests, and gradient boosting) are applied to build the prediction model. Feature engineering is employed to create meaningful input variables from raw data.

While flight distance and departure delay give contextual information about the route, age and gender offer demographic insights. The standard of services received throughout the flight is reflected in the following areas: cleanliness, baggage handling, and in-flight entertainment. Arrival delay affects the whole travel experience, which further reduces satisfaction. The characteristics of a passenger's travel and their degree of service are indicated by their customer type, travel type, and class type.
